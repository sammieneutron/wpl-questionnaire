DRAFT QUESTIONNAIRE FOR SELECTION OF MOST COST-CONSCIOUS OPERATOR IN THE UPSTREAM SECTOR OF NIGERIAN OIL AND GAS INDUSTRY

The Ministry of Petroleum Resources through the Nigerian Content Development and Monitoring Board (NCDMB), in collaboration with other Agencies in the Oil & Gas Industry wishes to institute awards for most cost-conscious operators in the Upstream Sector of the Nigerian Oil and Gas Industry.

The questions as contained in this questionnaire are intended to assist us to obtain vital information for the awards.

Please, answer each question to the best of your knowledge, your response will be kept in strict confidence as it is only for the purpose of the awards.
